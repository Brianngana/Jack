Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I book a tour?
You can book a tour by filling the booking form and making payment via M-PESA with the code provided.
Can I get a refund?
Yes, but only if you cancel at least 48 hours before the tour departure date.
Is the price inclusive of meals and transport?
Yes, our package prices include transport, accommodation, and meals unless stated otherwise.
How will I know my booking is confirmed?
You will receive a confirmation message or email after successful booking and payment.
